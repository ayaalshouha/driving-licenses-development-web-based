STEPS BY PROCESS

Driving License Process

Step 1

Complete the driver's license application form.

Step 2

Pay the license fee based on license class determined

Step 3

Schadule an appointment for each test (vision test, written test, street test).

Step 4

You have to pass each test in order to schadule an appointment for the other

Step 5

Issue your license.
